Smart Study Framework

We have created a system called the Smart Study Framework!

It has two main goals:

First, to turn students’ frustration of "I don’t understand this, I don’t enjoy studying" into "I can do this, studying is fun!" and make studying as easy and enjoyable as playing a game.

And they’ll do this all by themselves, without the help of a tutor or anyone else.

We’ll show and teach them techniques on how they can enjoy studying.

When someone is able to do something really well on their own and gets praise for it, they get even more interested in doing it better. They want to improve and get even more compliments and rewards (or awards). Once they get hooked on this, they want to keep getting better!

The reason why someone struggles or fails is because they have a “knowledge gap.” And a knowledge gap leads to a “skills gap.”

If someone can identify their knowledge gap and figure out how to fill it, nothing can stop them!

Our goal is to teach students how to identify their own knowledge gaps and find ways to fill those gaps by themselves.

We teach this through our Effortless Study Blueprint technique, which we share in our WhatsApp group called Smart Study Circle on EffortlessStudy.com.

So, what’s in this technique?

We use a special method with artificial intelligence that turns students’ frustration of “I don’t get it” into the joy of “I got it!” This makes studying feel like playing a fun game.

What do they need for this?

They need to join the Smart Study Circle WhatsApp group. Besides that, they’ll need a smartphone, internet access, and free access to artificial intelligence tools.
